Intro

In a 90s Fosters Beer Commercial a man is sitting in front of a TV. Another man throws a boomerang and hit the first man. Without any impulse the man switches the TV channel. The Claim: Boomerang - Australian for remote control

Boomerang - Australian for remote control is a remote control operated by simple gestures. The primary goal was to do a tiny application in Vue to get a first impression and help a friend at the same time.
